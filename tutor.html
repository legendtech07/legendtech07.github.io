Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about being a tutor

How do I become a tutor?

Download the Legend Tech Tutor App, register with your details, and start creating courses. Your application will be reviewed within 24-48 hours.

How do I earn Tutor Points (TP)?

Earn TP when students unlock your courses (10 TP per unlock), receive likes (1 TP per like), and through special promotions and contests.

When can I withdraw my earnings?

You can request withdrawal once you have at least 1000 TP ($10). Withdrawals are processed within 24-48 hours via PayPal, bank transfer, or crypto.

Is the app free to download?

Yes! The Legend Tech Tutor App is completely free to download and use. We only charge a small 10% commission on your earnings.

What subjects can I teach?

You can teach any tech-related subject: Programming, Web Development, Ethical Hacking, AI & Machine Learning, Design, Trading, Business, and more.

Do I need teaching experience?

No formal teaching experience required. If you have expertise in your field and can explain concepts clearly, you can be a great tutor!
